Prosthetics

At FAYZ DENT, prosthetics is a comprehensive approach to restoring jaw function and the aesthetics of your smile. We offer everything from removable and fixed prostheses to ultra-thin cosmetic veneers and bridges.

Our specialists use CAD/CAM technology for precise scanning, virtual design, and 3D fabrication to ensure perfect results.

We work only with certified laboratories and the latest biocompatible materials: ceramic, zirconia dioxide, acrylic, and high-quality composites.

  • Fixed solutions: full-ceramic veneers and bridges.
  • Removable prostheses: buccal and acrylic models.
  • Implant-supported restorations: from All-on-4 to full-arch rehabilitations.
  • CAD/CAM planning: high precision and rapid production.
  • Biocompatible materials: zirconia, ceramics, composites.
  • Custom shade and shape selection.
  • Post-prosthetic support: follow-up and adjustments.
